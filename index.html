CSCE 445/656: Computers and New Media

After taking an engineering ethics course this past summer, I wanted to enroll in a course that touched upon topics related to computational media regarding ownership and intellectual property. This class also offers a chance to do investigations and discussions concerning human-computer interaction, hypertext, media studies, and computers and the humanities by stepping away from code.
